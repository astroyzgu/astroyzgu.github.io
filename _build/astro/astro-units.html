单位转化

AB星等，ST星等，VEGA星等

The zero points are defined to coincide with the zero point of the widely used “visual” or standard “broad-band” V magnitude system:

\(m_\lambda (5500Å) = m_\nu (5500 Å) = V = 0\)

magnitude of V “broad-band”

flux of V “broad-band”

Definition

\(Mag_{ST} = m_\lambda(5500 Å) = 0\)

\(f_{\lambda} = 3.631×10^{−11} ergs\ s^{−1} Å^{−1} cm^{−2} \)

\(m_\lambda(\lambda) = -2.5 \times log(F_\lambda) - 21.1 \)

\(Mag_{AB} = m_\nu (5500 Å) = 0\)

\(f_\nu= 3.636×10^{−20} ergs\ s^{−1} Hz^{−1} cm^{−2}\)

\(m_\nu (\nu) = -2.5 \times log(F_\nu) - 48.6 \)
