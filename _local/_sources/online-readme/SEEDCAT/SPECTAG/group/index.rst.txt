group
==============

Group catalogs are split into two independent footprint region, NGC or SGC. 

Each ``{FOOTPRINT}`` (NGC or SGC) generates multiple files: 

  *  :doc:`{FOOTPRINT}_galaxy <_galaxy>`: summary of galaxy catalog
  *  :doc:`{FOOTPRINT}_group <_group>`: summary of group catalog
  *  :doc:`{FOOTPRINT}_1 <_1>`: membership galaxy
  *  :doc:`{FOOTPRINT}_2 <_2>`: membership group

{FOOTPRINT}_galaxy
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

:Summary: group catalog 
:Naming Convention: ``{FOOTPRINT}_galaxy.txt``,  where ``{FOOTPRINT}`` is the footprint region NGC or SGC. 
:File Type: ASCII


{FOOTPRINT}_group
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

:Summary: group catalog 
:Naming Convention: ``{FOOTPRINT}_group.txt``,  where ``{FOOTPRINT}`` is the footprint region NGC or SGC. 
:File Type: ASCII
